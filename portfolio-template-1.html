Seven Deadly Sins

Description :

Greed: is symbolized by a character hoarding and counting money, illustrating the insatiable desire for material wealth and possessions.

Envy: Envy is portrayed as a group of people looking longingly at others who possess what they desire, representing jealousy and covetousness

Gluttony: Gluttony is depicted as a man consuming food and drink excessively, surrounded by various culinary delights. This represents the overindulgence in earthly pleasures, particularly food and drink.

Lust: Lust is portrayed as a couple entwined in an intimate embrace, representing the excessive and uncontrollable desire for sensual pleasure.

Pride: Pride is symbolized by a character with an inflated ego, often depicted at the top of a tower or structure, looking down on others, reflecting excessive self-importance.

Sloth: Sloth is represented by a figure asleep or lethargic, failing to fulfill their responsibilities or engage in productive activities.

Wrath: Wrath is depicted as a scene of violence and chaos, with people engaged in aggressive acts, reflecting uncontrolled anger and aggression.
